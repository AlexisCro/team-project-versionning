UltimateRace Simulation is a car racing simulation game. It was developed by our team of racing and video game enthusiasts. We wanted to create a racing simulation game that was as realistic as possible. So we modelled the cars and circuits as realistically as possible. We also wanted the game to be accessible to everyone.

  • Who is it for? For all motor racing and video game fans
  • Category : SimRacer
  • Available platforms : PC (Steam), PS4/5 et XBOX
  • Languages : French, English, Deutsch, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese
  • Recommended PC configuration :
    • OS : Windows 10 64 bits
    • Processor : Intel Core i5-9600K ou AMD Ryzen 5 2600X
    • RAM : 16 Go
    • Graphic card : Nvidia GeForce GTX 1660 or AMD Radeon RX 590
    • Disk space : 50 Go
    • Steering wheel and pedals recommended
